Beer Glazed Brats and Sauerkraut

Sauerkraut and beer mixed with sausage

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle lager beer
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp dry mustard powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp dry dill weed
  • 1 lb fresh bratwurst sausages
  • 1 lb sauerkraut, drained

Directions:

  1. Mix beer, brown sugar, dry mustard, onion powder, pepper, and dill together in a bowl until brown sugar is dissolved.
  2. Place brats into a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Pour beer mixture over top and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Uncover and increase the heat to medium. Boil the sauce until it reduces to a thick, syrupy liquid, about 20 minutes. Tilt the skillet to roll brats in the sticky sauce until thoroughly coated. Transfer brats to a plate and cover to keep warm.
  4. Add sauerkraut to the skillet and stir to combine with any leftover sauce; cook for 5 to 8 minutes. Transfer sauerkraut to a serving platter and top with warm brats.
